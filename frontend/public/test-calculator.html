Component Functionality

The Probability Calculator component provides:

  • Calculation of probabilities for various distributions
  • Visual representation of probability areas
  • Calculation history tracking
  • Support for different probability types (less than, greater than, between, exactly)

Supported Distributions

  • Normal (Gaussian) Distribution
  • Binomial Distribution
  • Poisson Distribution
  • Exponential Distribution
  • Uniform Distribution
